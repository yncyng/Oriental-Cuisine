About Us

Welcome to
Oriental Cuisines Co.

At Oriental Cuisines Co., we are passionate about bringing the vibrant flavors of the East to your special occasions. With a commitment to authenticity and excellence, we craft exquisite Asian-inspired dishes that celebrate tradition while embracing modern tastes.

Whether it’s a grand celebration or an intimate gathering, our dedicated team ensures a seamless catering experience with exceptional service and carefully curated menus. Let us take your event to the next level with flavors that tell a story and a dining experience to remember.

At Oriental Cuisines Co., we believe that perfection is achieved through dedication, precision, and a deep respect for culinary heritage. Every dish we prepare is a masterpiece, crafted with the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques to ensure an unparalleled dining experience. From the delicate balance of spices in our Sichuan-inspired dishes to the meticulous preparation of our sushi and dim sum, we leave no detail overlooked in our pursuit of excellence.

Our commitment to exquisite taste goes beyond just flavor—it’s about evoking memories, creating emotions, and transporting our guests to the heart of Asia with every bite. Whether it’s the rich umami of Japanese miso broth, the fiery kick of Korean gochujang, or the comforting warmth of a Taiwanese braised pork dish, each recipe is designed to honor its origins while delighting the modern palate. At Oriental Cuisines Co., authenticity and innovation exist in perfect harmony.

Behind our refined expertise is a team of culinary artisans, each specializing in different facets of Asian cuisine. Our chefs have trained under master chefs in Japan, Korea, Taiwan, and China, bringing decades of experience to our kitchen. Every plate is thoughtfully designed, ensuring that our guests not only enjoy exceptional flavors but also experience the artistry, precision, and cultural significance behind each dish.

The heart of our vision lies with our founder and CEO, Mr. Stiffler Yanic Yang, a passionate visionary dedicated to redefining Asian cuisine on the global stage. With a deep-rooted appreciation for his heritage and a drive to innovate, Mr. Yang established Oriental Cuisines Co. to bring the finest flavors of the East to the world. His mission is to blend tradition with modern refinement, ensuring that every dining experience is not just a meal, but a journey through Asia’s rich culinary landscapes.

At Oriental Cuisines Co., we don’t just serve food—we craft experiences. Whether it’s an elegant wedding, a corporate banquet, or an intimate dinner, our goal remains the same: to create unforgettable moments through exceptional cuisine. Join us in celebrating the timeless beauty of Asian flavors, where every dish tells a story, and every bite is a tribute to perfection.
